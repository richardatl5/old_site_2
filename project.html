The first major goal is to read a book every week. Areas of topics can be business, economics, technology and personal improvement. To source for quality books to read, I will compile a list of books from known entreprenuers such as Bill Gates, Ray Dalio and other big personalities such as poliiticians who post a books to read list on a yearly basis. To facilitate this habit of reading frequently, I have purchased a kindle to access books on the go anytime.
It might not be realistic to meet the deadline of reading a book every week, so flexibility is available. For accountability's sake I have mentioned this goal to a close group of friends and posting this goal online for future reference. By the end of this goal I hope to achieve a better understanding on the major influences of our world from the impact of money to human nature.
